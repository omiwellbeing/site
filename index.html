Why Counselling

Barbara Sharon

As a dedicated counsellor, I focus on self-development, confidence-building, and life management, with a particular emphasis on helping individuals who have recently relocated abroad and struggle with life transition and adjustment issues. With over 10 years of experience and a deep understanding of the expatriate journey, I provide a safe and empathetic space for you to explore your goals, overcome obstacles, and create a life that aligns with your aspirations. I also offer the convenience of counselling in your native language, whether it’s English, Portuguese, Spanish, Italian, or French, to ensure you feel most comfortable during our sessions. Let’s embark on this empowering journey together towards self-discovery, resilience, and the fulfilment you deserve. Contact me today to take the first step toward a brighter future.
